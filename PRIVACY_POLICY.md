# Privacy Policy for Image Collector Bot

Effective Date: July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Image Collector Bot ("we", "our", or "the Bot") handles data obtained through the Pinterest API. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
The Bot requests access to your Pinterest account strictly via the official Pinterest OAuth 2.0 authentication process. 
* We only request the minimum required permissions (`boards:read` and `pins:read`).
* The Bot accesses your boards and pins solely for the purpose of retrieving image URLs and titles to organize them within the application.
* We do not collect, read, or store your personal profile information, passwords, or direct messages.

### 2. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure
We respect your privacy. We do not share, sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer any data obtained from your Pinterest account to any third parties. All processed image links are used exclusively for the core functionality of the Bot and remain strictly confidential.

### 3. Data Storage and Security
All access tokens and retrieved image URLs are stored securely. Access tokens are encrypted and used automatically by the Bot to maintain the connection with the Pinterest API. You can revoke the Bot's access at any time directly through your Pinterest account security settings.

### 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
